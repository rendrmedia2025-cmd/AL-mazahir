# Requirements Document

## Introduction

Al Mazahir Trading Est. requires a professional, trust-driven corporate website to showcase their industrial trading and safety equipment capabilities. The website will serve as a digital catalog and lead generation platform for B2B customers in Saudi Arabia and the GCC region, replacing traditional printed brochures while establishing credibility and driving inquiries.

## Glossary

- **Corporate_Website**: The main web application serving Al Mazahir Trading Est.
- **Product_Catalog**: Digital showcase of safety equipment and industrial supplies organized by categories
- **Inquiry_System**: Contact forms and communication channels for quote requests and business inquiries
- **Lead_Generation**: Process of capturing potential customer information through website interactions
- **B2B_Customer**: Business-to-business clients including procurement managers, construction companies, and industrial contractors
- **CTA**: Call-to-action buttons and elements that guide users toward desired actions

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Website Structure and Navigation

**User Story:** As a B2B customer, I want to easily navigate through the website sections, so that I can quickly find relevant information about Al Mazahir's services and products.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Corporate_Website SHALL display a single-page layout with clearly defined sections
2. WHEN a user scrolls through the page, THE Corporate_Website SHALL provide smooth navigation between sections
3. THE Corporate_Website SHALL include a hero section, about section, product categories, trust indicators, industries served, and contact section
4. WHEN a user accesses the website on mobile devices, THE Corporate_Website SHALL display a responsive layout optimized for mobile viewing
5. THE Corporate_Website SHALL load within 3 seconds on standard internet connections

### Requirement 2: Product Catalog Display

**User Story:** As a procurement manager, I want to browse product categories and view available items, so that I can identify suitable products for my business needs.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Product_Catalog SHALL organize products into six main categories: Safety Equipment, Fire & Safety, Construction & Building Materials, Tools & Machinery, Industrial Supplies, and Rental & Logistics Equipment
2. WHEN a user clicks on a product category, THE Product_Catalog SHALL display relevant products with images and descriptions
3. THE Product_Catalog SHALL include an "Enquire Now" button for each product category
4. WHEN displaying products, THE Product_Catalog SHALL show product images in a grid layout
5. THE Product_Catalog SHALL provide clear product descriptions for each category

### Requirement 3: Lead Generation and Inquiry System

**User Story:** As a potential customer, I want to request quotes and contact the company easily, so that I can get pricing information and establish business relationships.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Inquiry_System SHALL provide a contact form with fields for name, company, phone/WhatsApp, email, product requirement, and message
2. WHEN a user submits an inquiry, THE Inquiry_System SHALL send the information to Al Mazahir's business email
3. THE Inquiry_System SHALL include prominent "Request a Quote" and "Talk on WhatsApp" buttons
4. WHEN a user clicks the WhatsApp button, THE Inquiry_System SHALL open WhatsApp with a pre-filled message
5. THE Inquiry_System SHALL validate all required form fields before submission

### Requirement 4: Company Credibility and Trust Building

**User Story:** As a business decision maker, I want to understand Al Mazahir's capabilities and reliability, so that I can evaluate them as a potential supplier.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Corporate_Website SHALL display company information including years of experience and commitment to quality
2. THE Corporate_Website SHALL showcase trust indicators including wide product range, competitive pricing, reliable sourcing, on-time delivery, industry expertise, and quality assurance
3. THE Corporate_Website SHALL list the industries served: Construction, Oil & Gas, Manufacturing, Infrastructure, Warehousing, Logistics, and Government Projects
4. THE Corporate_Website SHALL display contact information including phone, email, and location in Saudi Arabia
5. THE Corporate_Website SHALL maintain a professional visual design with red/navy blue branding

### Requirement 5: Mobile Responsiveness and Performance

**User Story:** As a mobile user, I want to access the website seamlessly on my smartphone or tablet, so that I can browse products and contact the company while on the go.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Corporate_Website SHALL display properly on screen sizes from 320px to 1920px width
2. WHEN accessed on mobile devices, THE Corporate_Website SHALL maintain readable typography and accessible touch targets
3. THE Corporate_Website SHALL optimize images for fast loading across all devices
4. THE Corporate_Website SHALL implement mobile-first design principles
5. THE Corporate_Website SHALL provide the same functionality on mobile as on desktop

### Requirement 6: SEO and Discoverability

**User Story:** As a potential customer searching online, I want to find Al Mazahir's website through search engines, so that I can discover their services when looking for industrial suppliers.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Corporate_Website SHALL include SEO-optimized headings and meta descriptions
2. THE Corporate_Website SHALL implement schema markup for Local Business
3. THE Corporate_Website SHALL compress images for optimal loading speed
4. THE Corporate_Website SHALL integrate with Google Analytics for tracking
5. THE Corporate_Website SHALL be optimized for search engines to improve visibility

### Requirement 7: Content Management and Updates

**User Story:** As a business owner, I want to be able to update website content and product information, so that I can keep the website current with our latest offerings.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Corporate_Website SHALL use a content structure that allows for easy updates
2. THE Corporate_Website SHALL organize product information in a maintainable format
3. THE Corporate_Website SHALL support future expansion to multi-page structure
4. THE Corporate_Website SHALL be built with scalable architecture for future enhancements
5. THE Corporate_Website SHALL allow for integration with future e-commerce or ERP systems
